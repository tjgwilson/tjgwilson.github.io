Synthetic and Observed Hydrogen Emission from T Tauri Stars

Background

Accretion and outflows in CTTS control the stellar formation, but the underlying physics of these systems is poorly constrained. The use of high-resolution spectra offers a powerful diagnostic probe for understanding these processes. We present our first parameter study, which investigates synthetic and observed hydrogen emission.
