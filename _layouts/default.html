Summary

  • CTTSs are known for their hydrogen emission lines, magnetospheric accretion and mass outflow[1].

  • By comparing synthetic hydrogen profiles from the RT code TORUS[2] with observations of CTTSs, we aim to constrain and provide insight into the physics.

  • Our initial parameter study indicates that the existing line broadening mechanisms are insufficient to account for the observed hydrogen emission.
